About Us



Orchard Stables is a purpose-built Equestrian Centre which offers DIY, part or full livery for you and your horse.

The stables are situated on the outskirts of the quaint village of Collingham, just a stone's throw away from the market town of Newark and within easy access of the city of Lincoln and even Mablethorpe beach.

Orchard Stables is a livery yard that will endeavour to provide you with the sort of livery you and your horse deserve.

We are focused on providing a service with the highest levels of customer satisfaction – we will do everything we can to meet your expectations.

Take a look around our website and if you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

CHAPs

CHAPs, or Collingham Horse Adventure Park, is the first adventure park of its kind.
Try out the roller coaster, log roll, tunnel of love, water flume or the ghost train.
These are just a few in our exciting course of over eighteen obstacles!
