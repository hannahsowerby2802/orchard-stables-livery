About CHAPs



Collingham Horse Adventure Park, also known as CHAPs, is the first adventure park of its kind.

An adventure park for horses!

The park is a place to bring your horse or pony and have some fun either in-hand or ridden.
Try out the roller coaster, log roll, tunnel of love, water flume, ghost train and loads more.
There is an assortment of small cross country jumps and unusual obstacles. CHAPs is a place for you and your horse to have a go at:
horse agility, trail, TREC, natural horsemanship, or simply help bombproof your horse and help both of you gain confidence.
We welcome all disciplines, riding-instructors, mentors and both adults and children.

CHAPs is open from May until November, weather-dependant.

CHAPS can be hired for £15 per horse, or £10 per pony under 14hh.

To book CHAPs, or find out more information, please contact us.

Fancy Staying Over?



If you're travelling a long way to visit us, or simply wish to extend your trip to Collingham Horse Adventure Park, we offer facilities for you and your horse to stay over with us.

Orchard Stables is based in the village of Collingham, which is a quaint village near Newark. It offers brilliant hacking, both around the quiet village or through the nature reserve, which is just a 10-minute ride from the yard.
The village also has a local coop, cafe, fish and chip shop, restaurant and pub, all accessible via walking, riding or driving.

If you wish to venture further afield, we are also near Newark Castle, Lincoln Cathedral and Castle, Doddington Hall, Clumber Park, Sherwood Forest, Bransby Horse Rescue, and an hour's drive away from the beach.

Booking must be made in advance with a non-refundable deposit of £20 to be paid at the time of booking.
The remaining cost should be paid for either before arrival or on the morning of arrival.
For Basic Equine, please bring your own water bucket and poo-pick on your departure.
Premium Equine is subject to availability of the stables, due to primarily being a livery yard.
Arrival time: anytime after 9.00am - Departure time: before 5.00pm.
